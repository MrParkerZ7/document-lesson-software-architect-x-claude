## Overview

This section covers Business-to-Business (B2B) and Business-to-Consumer (B2C) identity scenarios.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand B2B collaboration patterns
- Learn B2C customer identity management
- Implement external identity solutions

## Key Concepts

### B2B (Business-to-Business)

External partner collaboration:
- Guest user management
- Federation with partner IdPs
- Cross-organization access

**Use Cases**: Partner portals, supplier collaboration, contractor access

### B2C (Business-to-Consumer)

Customer identity at scale:
- Self-service registration
- Social identity providers
- Custom branding
- Progressive profiling

**Use Cases**: E-commerce, customer portals, mobile apps

### Comparison

| Aspect | B2B | B2C |
|--------|-----|-----|
| Users | Partners, vendors | Customers |
| Scale | Thousands | Millions |
| Identity | Corporate IdPs | Social + local |
